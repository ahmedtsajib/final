About Us

Bangladesh is a small but beautiful country. There are many attractive places to visit all around the country. To disclose the descriptions of those attractive places as well as the instructions to go those places, this site is here as your helping hand. This site not only depicts the beauty of the beautiful places of Bangladesh but also provides the information about how to go those places, where to stay there, what cautionary measures should be taken and so on. We are also open to serve you with additional information or help so far we able to supply to satisfy your curiosity or necessity and you need not to pay anything to get help from our site. In a word, this site is a free travel guide to visit
Bangladesh.
